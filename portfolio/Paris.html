Our Story

Founded in 2025 by visionary entrepreneur Peris W. K., Paris Spa & Wellness was born from a profound belief in the transformative power of self-care. After years of observing the relentless pace of modern life, Peris envisioned a sanctuary where individuals could escape the demands of daily life and reconnect with their inner peace.

Our name pays homage to the timeless elegance and sophistication of Paris, while our approach combines ancient wellness traditions with cutting-edge techniques. Each treatment is meticulously crafted to deliver not just relaxation, but true transformation.

At Paris Spa & Wellness, we believe that luxury is not just about opulence, but about creating meaningful experiences that nurture the soul. Our expert therapists are dedicated to providing personalised care that addresses your unique needs and leaves you feeling renewed, revitalised, and radiant.
