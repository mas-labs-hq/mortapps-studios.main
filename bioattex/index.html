Install Bioattex

For the best experience with faster performance, offline access, and a native app feel, install Bioattex on your device.

Install Bioattex App

  1. Click the three dots at the top-right of your browser.
  2. Select "Cast, save, and share" then "Install page as app".
  3. A pop-up appears at the top: click "Install".
Done! Bioattex will now launch like a native application.

Install on iOS:

  1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
Done! Bioattex will appear on your home screen.
