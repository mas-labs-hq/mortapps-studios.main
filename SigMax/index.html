Web Development Services Agreement

Terms & Conditions for Website Development, Hosting, and Domain Management Services

Version 1.0 March 2026 Nairobi, Kenya labs@mortappsstudios.com

1. Introduction

This Web Development Services Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions governing the relationship between MortApps Studios ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our") and the Client ("you" or "your") for the provision of website development, hosting, and domain management services. By engaging our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by the terms set forth in this document.

1.1 Purpose of This Agreement

This Agreement is designed to establish clear expectations, protect the interests of both parties, and ensure a transparent, professional working relationship. We believe that clarity from the outset leads to successful partnerships and exceptional outcomes.

1.2 Scope of Services

Our services typically include, but are not limited to:

  • Website design and development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related technologies)
  • Web application development and deployment
  • Website hosting via GitHub Pages or similar platforms
  • Domain name registration and management
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) integration
  • Ongoing maintenance and updates (as agreed separately)

2. Hosting & Deployment

2.1 Hosting Platform

MortApps Studios hosts client websites on GitHub Pages or similar reputable hosting platforms. This approach offers reliability, security, and cost-effectiveness. By hosting on such platforms, the underlying code of your website may be publicly visible in repositories. This is a standard industry practice and does not compromise the security or functionality of your website.

2.2 Visibility of Project Files

We believe in transparency. Code hosted on public platforms like GitHub may be viewable by the public. This does not affect your website's operation, security, or exclusivity. Your website remains unique to your brand, and the public nature of the code repository is simply a technical characteristic of our chosen hosting infrastructure.

2.3 Hosting Continuity

MortApps Studios will maintain hosting services for the duration of our working relationship. In the event of service termination (as outlined in Section 6), hosting continuation shall be at our sole discretion. We reserve the right to remove or archive hosted projects following termination, subject to the file delivery obligations described herein.

3. SEO & Brand Attribution

3.1 Developer Attribution

As part of our standard practice, MortApps Studios embeds developer attribution and SEO metadata within client websites. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Meta tags identifying MortApps Studios as the developer
  • Footer credits or discrete branding elements
  • Structured data markup referencing our portfolio
  • Backlinks to our official website or portfolio

3.2 Purpose of Attribution

This attribution serves multiple purposes: it helps potential clients discover our services through search engines, establishes credibility for both your website and our portfolio, and maintains professional industry standards. By engaging our services, you consent to the inclusion of such attribution in your website.

3.3 Removal of Attribution

Should you wish to remove MortApps Studios attribution from your website following project completion or termination, you may do so at your discretion. However, we kindly request that attribution remain in place as a gesture of goodwill and professional courtesy.

Why This Matters: Developer attribution is an industry-standard practice that benefits both parties. It enhances your website's SEO through association with our established portfolio while allowing us to grow our client base through demonstrated work quality.

4. Domain Name Management

4.1 Domain Registration

Domain names for client projects are registered through MortApps Studios using reputable domain registrars (such as Truehost, Namecheap, or similar providers). The domain name is registered on your behalf, and you retain full ownership and rights to the domain name itself.

4.2 Registration Details

While we facilitate the domain registration process, the specific account credentials, registrar selection, and detailed purchase information remain within MortApps Studios' administrative control. This approach allows us to manage technical configurations efficiently and provide seamless service. We are not obligated to share registrar account details or purchase credentials with clients.

4.3 Client Access

Should you require direct access to manage your domain independently, we will provide reasonable guidance on the registrar platform used. However, setting up an independent account and transferring domain management is your responsibility should you choose to pursue that option.

4.4 Domain Renewal Reminder

MortApps Studios commits to providing a renewal reminder to clients approximately one (1) week before the domain name registration expires. This reminder will typically be sent via email, WhatsApp, or phone. The reminder is a courtesy service and is intended to help you maintain uninterrupted domain ownership.

4.5 Renewal Responsibility

Ultimate responsibility for domain renewal rests with you, the client. While we provide reminders, it is your obligation to ensure timely renewal payment. MortApps Studios is not liable for domain expiration, loss of domain name, or any consequences resulting from failure to renew on time.

4.6 Domain Expiration Consequences

In the event that a domain name expires due to non-renewal:

  • Service Disruption: Your website will become inaccessible via the domain name
  • Recovery Costs: Restoring an expired domain may incur additional fees from the registrar
  • Restoration Service: If you wish for MortApps Studios to assist in domain recovery, a restoration fee will apply, separate from the standard renewal cost
  • Potential Loss: Domains that remain expired for extended periods may be acquired by third parties, resulting in permanent loss

IMPORTANT: We strongly recommend maintaining your own calendar reminders for domain renewal dates. Our reminder service is provided as a courtesy, but we cannot guarantee delivery due to factors beyond our control.

5. Project Files & Intellectual Property

5.1 Ownership of Deliverables

Upon full payment for services rendered, you own the custom website content, design elements, and functionality created specifically for your project. This includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and any custom code developed for your website.

5.2 Third-Party Assets

Projects may incorporate third-party libraries, frameworks, or assets. These remain subject to their respective licenses. MortApps Studios retains the right to use general development patterns, templates, and code snippets in future projects.

5.3 File Delivery Upon Termination

In the event of service termination, MortApps Studios will provide you with all project source files. Delivery will typically be made via email or WhatsApp within a reasonable timeframe following termination confirmation.

6. Service Termination

6.1 Termination Scenarios

This Agreement may be terminated under the following circumstances:

  • Mutual Agreement: Both parties agree to conclude the working relationship
  • Completion of Project: All deliverables have been provided and accepted
  • Client Request: You choose to discontinue services for any reason
  • Developer Discretion: We reserve the right to terminate services for breach of Agreement, non-payment, or other material cause

6.2 Obligations Upon Termination

Upon termination, MortApps Studios will deliver all project source files, provide reasonable guidance on domain registration details, and fulfill any outstanding payment obligations on our end, if applicable.

6.3 Post-Termination Hosting

Continued hosting of your website on our GitHub infrastructure following termination is at our sole discretion. We may choose to maintain hosting as a courtesy, archive the repository, or remove it entirely. To ensure uninterrupted service, we recommend that clients establish independent hosting arrangements following termination.

6.4 Post-Termination Domain Management

We are not obligated to continue managing your domain name post-termination. We will inform you of the registrar platform used, but setting up independent domain management is your responsibility. Any future renewals, transfers, or modifications must be handled directly by you or through a new service provider.

7. Limitation of Liability

7.1 Service Availability

While we strive to maintain high availability and uptime for hosted websites, MortApps Studios cannot guarantee uninterrupted service. Factors beyond our control—including hosting platform outages, internet connectivity issues, and force majeure events—may affect service availability.

7.2 Domain Expiration

MortApps Studios is not liable for any damages, losses, or disruptions resulting from domain name expiration. This includes loss of business, revenue, data, or reputation. The renewal reminder we provide is a courtesy, not a guarantee.

7.3 Maximum Liability

In no event shall MortApps Studios' total liability exceed the total amount paid by you for the specific service giving rise to the claim. This limitation applies to all claims, whether based on contract, tort, or otherwise.

8. Client Responsibilities

To ensure a successful partnership, we ask that you:

  • Provide Timely Information: Supply all necessary content, images, and branding materials promptly
  • Review & Feedback: Provide feedback on deliverables within agreed timeframes
  • Payment: Fulfill all payment obligations as agreed
  • Domain Renewal: Ensure timely renewal of domain registration
  • Communication: Maintain updated contact information for renewal reminders and important notices
  • Independent Backup: Maintain your own copies of all delivered files and content

9. Amendments & Modifications

This Agreement may be amended or modified only by written consent of both parties. Any changes to the terms outlined herein must be documented and signed by authorized representatives of both MortApps Studios and the Client.

10. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya. In the event of any dispute arising from or relating to this Agreement, the parties shall first attempt to resolve the matter through good-faith negotiation. Should negotiation fail, the dispute may be submitted to the appropriate courts of Kenya.
